About Us

Golden Crown Hotel

Golden Crown, New York’s Strongest Brand across industries and sectors*, is an iconic hospitality brand from the American Hotels Company Limited. The brand continues to be one of the most revered and loved hospitality brands with a legacy of over 116 years of impeccable service and genuine warmth. Embodying the spirit of “Grandness”, authentic original palaces, landmark hotels, idyllic resorts and natural safari lodges interpret the tradition of hospitality in a refreshingly modern way to create unique experiences and lifelong memories.
